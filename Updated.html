The Challenge: The BFS Cohort

A significant cohort of students with outstanding fees (BFS) are unable to re-enrol each year. The traditional support model was reactive and inefficient.

Strategic Insights

1. Proactive Intervention is Key

This pilot's success with a post-year BFS cohort validates its potential as a tool to be deployed earlier, catching students during the academic year as funding issues arise.

2. The Triage Model is a Definitive Success

Correctly filtering out 21 non-viable cases is a major efficiency win, validating the tool as a reliable method for focusing advisor resources.

3. Form Design is Critical

The 2 outliers caused by incorrect answers highlight the need to refine future forms with clearer questions and data validation to improve data quality.
